# Operations Optimisation and Revenue Planning

## Recommended application use
Use this as a strong supporting project for Strategy & Analytics and Business Analytics roles.

## Summary
This folder contains optimisation models for business planning and resource allocation. It includes a rental fleet revenue optimisation model, a stadium assignment optimisation project, and an operations management optimisation workbook.

## Skills shown
Linear programming, Pyomo, Excel modelling, capacity planning, revenue optimisation, resource allocation, constraints, shadow prices, planning under limited capacity, operational decision-making.

## Files included
- `business_optimization_modeling_code.ipynb` — generator rental optimisation model.
- `revenue_capacity_optimization_notebook.ipynb` — optimisation modelling notebook.
- `revenue_capacity_optimization_report.html` — rendered optimisation report.
- `operations_management_optimization_workbook.xlsx` — operations management optimisation workbook.
